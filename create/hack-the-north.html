The Problem

Moving out for university or college comes with both freedom and additional responsibility. Students have busy, demanding schedules that are constantly changing, which demands good organization and time management. From school to work, from extra-curriculars to just living life, students can often become overwhlemed and stressed out with their schedules. As things start to pile up, it is easy to procrastinate, forget to do things, and end up eating ramen for dinner... again.


The Solution

By having one central app to organize and remind people of their tasks and deadlines, they can learn to manage their time better, resulting in increased productivity and less stress. For this design challenge, I designed a simple, effective solution, where users can easily keep track of their tasks, including upcoming assignment deadlines, co-op interviews, and even when to buy their groceries. The app features two view modes, Overview or Calendar, and a simple process for adding and managing tasks.

Design Style

The colour palette used for this design includes dark grey text on a light background to allow for contrast and better readability. The purple was used as the main brand colour in the headers and icons to include some colour without being too intrusive. The pink colour was used as a highlight to draw the userâ€™s attention to certain important visual elements, such as actions, date indicator, and High Priority indicator. The purple and pink complement each other, giving the app a cool, calming feeling, so as to not give users any more stress than they already have from their busy schedules.
